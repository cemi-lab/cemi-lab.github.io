Openings

We are always on the lookout for highly motivated postdoctoral fellows, Ph.D. students, master students, and research assistants interested in working on cutting-edge problems in computational electromagnetics and full-wave inversion. Interested applicants with an aptitude for fundamental research are encouraged to contact Prof. Han with a CV.

Postdoctoral Fellow Positions

1. Recruitment Plan
(1) Research areas: Computational electromagnetics, microwave imaging, electromagnetic full-wave inversion, geophysical exploration, etc.

2. Requirements
(1) The applicant is usually not more than 35 years old and is about to obtain or has obtained a Ph.D. degree in the past three years;
(2) The applicant has a good academic background and strong independent scientific research ability and preferably has published papers in high-level journals, e.g. IEEE TAP, IEEE TMTT, IEEE TGRS, and GJI, as the first author;
(3) The applicant has a strong ability to derive mathematical formulas and program computational electromagnetics code, e.g. fortran, matlab, python, etc., and also can assist the advisor in supervising master students;
(3) The applicant has good English reading and writing skills and good teamwork communication and coordination skills.

3. Salary
(1) The annual salary before tax is about 400,000-600,000 CNY which includes the local government postdoctoral living allowance;
(2) During the station, you can apply for research funding from the local government up to 200,000 CNY;
(3) During the station, you are supported to apply for research funding from China Postdoctoral Science Foundation and National Natural Science Youth Foundation;
(4) Postdoctoral fellows who stay in Dongguan to work after leaving the station can enjoy subsidies of up to 600,000 CNY.

4. Welfare
(1) Pay insurances and housing fund (endowment insurance, medical insurance, unemployment insurance, work-related injury insurance and maternity insurance, and housing provident fund);
(2) Can apply to stay in Zhenhai District Talent Apartment (doctoral students enjoy rent discounts);
(3) Enjoy other equal welfare benefits for faculty and staff of xxxx.

5. Materials Required for Postdoctoral Fellow Application
The applicant is required to send the following electronic documents to Prof. Han's email. The subject of the email should indicate "Applying for Postdoctoral Fellow + Name" or "博士后申请+姓名".
(1) A Chinese or English CV which includes a detailed list of published papers. The co-first authors and the corresponding authors must be clearly denoted;
(2) Three representative papers;
(3) Other materials that can demonstrate the applicant's research ability.

Ph.D. Positions

1. Recruitment plan
(1) Research areas: Computational electromagnetics, microwave imaging, electromagnetic full-wave inversion, geophysical exploration, etc.

2. PhD Programs
(1) Doctoral students are jointly trained by Eastern Institute for Advanced Study (EIAS) and Shanghai Jiao Tong University (1+3) or The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (2+2), with a dual supervision system, and will be awarded doctoral degrees from Shanghai Jiao Tong University or The Hong Kong Polytechnic University after graduation.

   

3. Training Modes
(1) EIT-SJTU Joint PhD Program: The PhD students under the EIT-SJTU joint PhD Program to register their status at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, and they are trained by both parties and guided by dual tutors. In the first year, they study the courses at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. Since the second year, they are mainly engaged in scientific research related to the academic dissertations in Eastern Institute for Advanced Study. If the students under joint PhD Program meet the graduation conditions of Shanghai Jiao Tong University, they will be awarded the graduation certificate of SJTU. If the students meet the requirements of Shanghai Jiao Tong University on doctoral degree, they will be granted with the doctoral degree of SJTU. In order to stimulate the scientific research and innovation of doctoral students, we offer competitive scholarship and grants for joint doctoral students during their studies.

(2) EIT-PolyU Joint PhD Program: During the two-year study in Hong Kong, the PhD students under joint PhD Program will be entitled of the same amount of scholarship and grants as the PhD students of Hong Kong Polytechnic University. During your study at EIAS, EIAS will provide you with competitive scholarship and grants according to relevant national regulations so that you can concentrate on your study and research. If the students meet the requirements of Hong Kong Polytechnic University on doctoral degree, they will be granted with the doctoral degree of PolyU.

4. Links
Details and application requirements can be found in the following links:
(1) EIT-SJTU Joint PhD Program: https://www.eias.ac.cn/?p=668&cats=74
(2) EIT-PolyU Joint PhD Program: https://www.eias.ac.cn/?p=410&cats=74

5. Contact
(1) Candidates can send their resumes, transcripts, scientific research achievements, etc. to the Prof. Shang's email, and the subject of the email should indicate "Apply for PhD + name" or "博士申请+姓名".
